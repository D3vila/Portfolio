About Me

My story starts at Grand Canyon, Arizona. Ever since I was young, I always was in to gaming and computers growing up. My first video game system was a Nintendo GameBoy. As a kid, I would always wonder how this small box was able to have a world inside it. As my brother and I got older, we would spend hours playing games trying to get the cheat codes (for blood-mode in Sega Genesis Mortal Kombat it was A-B-A-C-A-B-B) and mess with all the setting and see what we could do to mess with the game.

As I got older, I was re-introduced to computer programing in high school. I was enrolled in a basic computer class and I remember enjoying myself with the HTML section of class. I love how you were able to change and make the page how ever you want it to be. In college, is where I built my first website. It was all HTML but it was the first time I knew I had a passion for programming.

I currently live in Phoenix, Arizona with my beautiful wife, our soon to be daughter, and three dogs. COVID allowed me to step back and gave me an opportunity to pursue a passion I've always had. It's a satifiying feeling when you are able to take an idea and make it a tangle product. Coding has been a resource to tap into my creative and problem-solving side. I think back to how far I have come to where I am now is an amazing transformation that I am proud of. From console.log ("Hello World") to building full stack websites is amazing feeling and I am ready to see what doors open for me in the future.
