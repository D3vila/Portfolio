Portfolio

Some of my projects that I have worked on

The following projects demonstrate my knowledge of the stacks and languages I have learned from my studies at App Academy. Some of the projects are from Solo built and other are in a team built projects.

DeLorean Traveler

Delorean Traveler is an app based on Airbnb website that allows users to reserve the Delorean to travel to movie destinations in time. Users can also leave reviews on locations they have visited and have many locations to choose from. DeLorean Traveler was created with a Python and Flask/SQLAlchemy backend that interacts with a PostgreSQL database and a React/Redux frontend.

  • Travel to the future or past

Mario Kart High Score

Mario Kart High Score is an app based on the Map My Run website that allows users to record their race times for their favorite Mario Kart courses and add comments about their race experience. Mario Kart High Score was created using Flask, React, and Redux.

  • can you beat some track times

AirBnBoo

A clone of AirBnB is a place where people can book their paranormal/haunted stay at one of the popular haunted locations. Users can book a stay and leave reviews after their stay. AirBnBoo was built with express, PostgreSQL,and sequelize in the backend. React/Redux, Javascript in the frontend.

  • Book a haunted stay

Marvelous

Marvelous is a clone of Goodreads app, where Marvel fans can review and rate their favorite Marvel Movies to other users. Marvelous was built with Express, PostgreSQL, and Sequelize backend. The frontend was built with PUG, JavaScript, and CSS.
